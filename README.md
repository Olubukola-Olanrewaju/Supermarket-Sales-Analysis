# Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing supermarket sales data to uncover insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and branch performance.

The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support business decision making.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Microsoft Excel  

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains 1000 rows and 17 columns, including:

Invoice ID, Branch, City, Customer Type, Gender, Product Line, Unit Price, Quantity, Tax, Sales, Date, Time, Payment, COGS, Gross Income, Rating.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Overview
- Total sales, profit, and orders  
- Monthly sales trend  
- Sales by branch  
- Sales by hour  
- Customer rating distribution  

### 2. Product Performance
- Sales by product line  
- Profit by product line  
- Quantity sold by product  
- Product sales contribution  

### 3. Customer Insights
- Sales by gender  
- Sales by customer type  
- Preferred payment method  
- Sales by hour  

### 4. Branch Performance
- Sales comparison by branch  
- Profit by branch  
- Monthly trend by branch  
- Customer rating by branch  

---

## Key Insights
- Branch C generates the highest sales and profit  
- Food and Beverages lead in both revenue and quantity sold  
- Evening hours show higher customer activity  
- Members contribute more revenue than normal customers  
- Ewallet is the most preferred payment method
